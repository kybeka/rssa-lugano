Who we are

2026
Founded

The Russian Speaking Student Association brings together students who share a language and a curiosity for culture — at one of Europe's most international universities.

Located in Lugano — where Italy, Switzerland, and the world converge — RSSA creates a space to connect, celebrate culture, and build lasting friendships. We are open to every member of the USI community: language is a meeting place, not a dividing line.

Community-driven

Every event is planned by students, for students.

Non-profit

Run entirely on goodwill and student energy.

Open to all

Any USI student is welcome, regardless of background.

"Language and culture
are not threats,
but bridges."

In Lugano — at the intersection of Swiss precision and Mediterranean warmth — we choose connection over division, and curiosity over isolation.
